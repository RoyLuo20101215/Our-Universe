Hera Mission Launch

October 7, 2024

The European Space Agency launched its Hera mission, aiming to study the Didymos-Dimorphos asteroid system. This mission follows NASA's DART mission, which successfully impacted the smaller moonlet of the system in 2022. Hera will investigate the aftermath of this impact, providing valuable data on the behavior of asteroids and their deflection capabilities.

Hunter's Supermoon

The full moon on October 17, 2024, also known as the Hunter's Moon, was a supermoon, making it the largest and brightest of the year. Supermoons occur when the Moon is closest to Earth in its orbit, resulting in an impressive sight as it rose at sunset and remained visible throughout the night.
